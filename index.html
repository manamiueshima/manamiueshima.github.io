Manami Ueshima

Bio

Manami Ueshima is a board-certified pediatrician in Japan with a keen interest in pediatric neurology. She is currently a full-time MPH student at the Johns Hopkins University Bloomberg School of Public Health. Her focus is on the neurological complications of COVID-19 in children, motivated by her own experience with cerebrospinal fluid (CSF) leakage due to COVID-19. She aspires to pursue a career in pediatric neurology in a clinical setting while also conducting research related to child health.
